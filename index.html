              Guide

              Welcome to Tracktifier! created by Metillo and Yano!

              Tracktifier is your ultimate task management tool designed to help you stay organized and on top of your deadlines. Whether you're a student juggling multiple assignments, a professional managing projects, or anyone looking to boost productivity, Tracktifier simplifies your task management experience. With a user-friendly interface and essential features, you can easily add, track, and manage your tasks effortlessly.
              Adding a Task
              To get started, simply click on the "Add a Task" section. Fill in the task name, select the deadline, choose the time, and specify the type of task. Tracktifier allows you to categorize your tasks into large projects, medium tasks, small tasks, or group tasks, ensuring you have a clear view of what needs to be done. Once you've filled in the details, click the "Add Task" button to save it. Your tasks will automatically appear in the relevant sections!
              Managing Your Tasks
              With Tracktifier, managing your tasks is a breeze. You can easily view your tasks organized by deadlines, from today’s to upcoming weeks. Mark tasks as done when you've completed them, and they'll move to the Done Tasks section. If you miss a deadline, don’t worry! Your missed tasks will be categorized for easy access. Plus, you can delete any task you no longer need by clicking the Delete button next to it.
              Stay On Track with Notifications
              One of the standout features of Tracktifier is its notification system. As deadlines approach, Tracktifier will send you real-time notifications to remind you of upcoming tasks. You can customize reminders based on the type of task to ensure you never miss a deadline. This way, you can focus on what matters most while Tracktifier takes care of the reminders!
              Explore Tabs for Organized Viewing
              Navigate through the different tabs—Today, Next Week, Next Month, Done Tasks, Missed Tasks, and Upcoming Tasks—to manage your workload effectively. Each tab provides a clear view of your tasks, helping you prioritize what needs your attention right away. Enjoy the seamless organization that Tracktifier offers, making your task management experience both efficient and enjoyable!
